French Toast

Perfect French Toast

Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Maple Syrup
  • Honey
  • Fruits aka [strawberry, blueberry, etc

Steps
  1. Crack Eggs into bowl
  2. Mix in Milk
  3. Mix in Sugar
  4. Mix in Cinnamon
  5. Warm Pan to Medium Heat
  6. Cut fresh fruits
  7. Dip Bread Slice into Egg mixture and coat entirely
  8. Cook on Pan
  9. Top with Butter, Fruit, Maple Syrup, or Honey
  10. EAT DAT BITCH
