Gumbo Feast

Ingredients

  • Small whole raw chicken
  • Andouille Suausage
  • Lard or Butter
  • White Flour
  • Rice
  • Celery
  • Onions
  • Bellpepper
  • Gumbo File aka [sassafrass]
  • Cajun Seasoning aka [Tony Chachare's]
  • optional seasoning aka [cayenne, salt, pepper etc]

Steps
  1. Warm 1/2 cup butter or lard medium heat
  2. Add 1:1 Flour to preheated oil [1/2 cup]
  3. Stir slowly until achieving a dark brown color
  4. Boil Small whole chicken
  5. Bring 2:1 Water to Rice mixture to boil, cover
  6. Bring heat to low
  7. Cut Andoille Suasuage into Slices
  8. Cut Veggies aka [Celery, Onion, Bellpepper]
  9. After boiling chicken, remove meat and let cool
  10. Shred chicken from bones
  11. Brown Andouille Sausage in pan
  12. Bring broth to a Boil
  13. Add dark brown Roux to boiling broth
  14. Add Andouille and drippings to broth
  15. Add Bellpeppers
  16. Add Onions
  17. add Celery
  18. Add Cajun Seasoning and File to taste
  19. ALlow Gumbo to simmer for a bit longer
  20. Serve Gumbo over scoop of rice
  21. EAT DAT BITCH
